Privacy Policy for KO-UZ

Effective date: March 25, 2026

KO-UZ is a vocabulary and dictionary learning application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We may process words, phrases, search queries, and text entered by users in order to provide dictionary, translation, explanation, and vocabulary learning features.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless it is provided by the user as part of app input.

2. How We Use Information

We use information only to operate and improve app features, including:

  • showing word meanings or translations,
  • providing explanations,
  • saving vocabulary for learning purposes,
  • improving speed through temporary caching.

3. Data Storage

Some user-entered content may be stored temporarily or persistently to support saved vocabulary, faster responses, and better app performance.

4. Third-Party Services

KO-UZ may use third-party services such as hosting, analytics, cloud infrastructure, or translation-related providers when necessary to operate the app.

5. Children's Privacy

KO-UZ does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information processed by the app, but no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
